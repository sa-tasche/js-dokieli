dokieli

dokieli is a clientside editor for decentralised article publishing, annotations, and social interactions.

Introduction

dokieli enables individuals and communities, to share, publish, and annotate articles using their preferred identities and storage, all under their control. All content produced by dokieli can be used with other standard-compliant applications, so you are always free to switch to another application and take your content with you.

This webpage is a dokieli article. Try it now!

dokieli enables the Write dimension of the Read-Write Web.

Features

  • Share

    Share articles or specific parts with your contacts or groups. More

  • Annotation

    Annotate articles with comments, bookmarks, highlights, and more. More

  • Citations

    Add robust, contextual citations and notify cited entities. More

  • Inline Charts

    Search for statistical data and display inline-charts to support sound argumentation and verifiable data journalism. More

  • Geo and Statistical Data

    Enhance your outdoor activities and stories by importing and showing maps with detailed GPS stats, health metrics, and other information. More

  • Web Feeds

    Generate and publish web feeds of your articles. More

  • Archive and Robust Links

    Make links more resilient to changes and disappearances, ensuring they preserve context, meaning, and content integrity. More

  • Technical Specifications

    Write technical specifications, view conformance requirements and test coverage.

  • Digital Rights

    Check the compatibility of a storage's data policies with your preferred policies.

  • Access Request

    Request access to documents and data.

  • Audience Occupation

    Match an article's audience with your CV's occupations and be notified of information of interest.
